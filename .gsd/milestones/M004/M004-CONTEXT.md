# M004 Context: Claims, Billing & Practice Management

## Purpose

M004 closes the revenue cycle for a solo PT practice. M001-M003 delivered a complete clinical documentation platform: patients, scheduling, PT note types, objective measures, AI voice-to-note, PDFs, fax, and authorisation tracking. What was still missing after M003 was the financial layer — a PT practice cannot operate without billing Medicare and commercial payers, and currently that work happens outside MedArc in a separate billing system.

M004 makes MedArc the single system of record for both clinical documentation and revenue cycle management. After M004 a solo PT has no operational reason to use a second tool for day-to-day practice management.

**Free Exercise DB** — Public domain exercise library (https://github.com/wrkout/exercises.json). Contains 800+ exercises with images, descriptions, body part tags, and category tags. Shipped as a compressed JSON file inside the app bundle (`resources/exercise_db.json.gz`). Loaded into the `exercise_library` SQLCipher table at first launch via a seeding migration.

The exercise images are referenced by relative path within the JSON. On first launch the images are extracted to the app's data directory and the `exercise_library.image_path` column is updated to the absolute local path.

## Regulatory Context

### 8-Minute Rule
Medicare requires at least 8 minutes of direct one-on-one timed service to bill one unit (15-minute code). The rule is: total timed minutes ÷ 15, where the remainder determines whether an additional unit is billable (≥8 min = 1 additional unit). Commercial payers typically follow the AMA rule (round to nearest 15 minutes). MedArc will support both rule sets with per-payer configuration.

### Therapy Cap
Medicare caps outpatient PT and speech-language pathology services at $2,480 per beneficiary per calendar year (2024 figure; updated annually). Charges above this cap still require the KX modifier to certify medical necessity. At $3,000 cumulative charges, the claim enters the Targeted Medical Review programme and requires additional documentation.

### KX Modifier
Added to a CPT code when cumulative Medicare PT charges for the patient in the calendar year exceed the therapy cap threshold. Attestation that the services are medically necessary and that clinical documentation supports continued treatment. MedArc auto-applies this based on the running cumulative total in `therapy_cap_index`.

### CQ Modifier
Required on all PT timed CPT codes when a Physical Therapist Assistant (PTA) performs more than 10% of the service. The PTA billing role is configured per provider account. MedArc auto-applies CQ when the treating provider's role is set to PTA.

### ABN (Advance Beneficiary Notice of Noncoverage)
Required when a provider believes Medicare may deny a claim. Must be given to the patient before the service with an estimate of the cost. MedArc generates the CMS-R-131 ABN form pre-populated from the encounter data.

### MIPS (Merit-based Incentive Payment System)
CMS quality reporting programme for Medicare clinicians. PT-relevant measures: #128 (BMI), #134 (PHQ-2/9), #155 (Falls risk), #182 (Functional outcome assessment — LEFS/DASH/NDI/Oswestry), #217-222 (Functional status measures). Performance data feeds into the CMS Quality Payment Programme submission portal. MedArc generates the performance rate table and a CMS-compatible export.

## Office Ally Integration

Office Ally is the most widely used low-cost clearinghouse for solo PT practices. The integration uses:
- **SFTP upload**: Claims files uploaded to `ftp.officeally.com:/edi/claims/` using the practice's EDI credentials stored in macOS Keychain
- **Acknowledgement polling**: Office Ally returns 997/999 functional acknowledgements via SFTP; MedArc polls every 30 minutes for new files in the `/edi/acks/` path
- **ERA retrieval**: 835 files deposited in `/edi/era/` are downloaded and processed by the `import_era_835` command

SFTP credentials (host, username, private key path) are stored in macOS Keychain using the existing `keyring` crate (already in Cargo.toml). The private key passphrase is protected by Touch ID via the existing biometric authentication layer from M003/S01.
